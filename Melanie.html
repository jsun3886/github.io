MA, LPC

As a therapist, my job is to help you confront the difficult, be true to yourself, and untangle the influences of past relationships and trauma* on your life. I typically use family systems to identify where your conflicts have developed, cognitive behavioral therapy to identify various perspectives and encourage more flexible thinking, and I use EMDR and parts work to help explore the more difficult challenges to your happiness. For those who would like to do this exploration through a creative means I also offer Art Therapy.
I would characterize my style as direct but kind. Life is hard, challenging, and stressful. Allow me to help you with some of your burdens. Reach out today to see available appointments.
